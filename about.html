About Our School

Established 2016 B.S. · Satyawati-6, Johang, Gulmi

Shree Saraswati Secondary School is located in Satyawati Rural Municipality-6, Johang, Gulmi, Nepal. Since our founding we have been committed to providing quality education guided by our motto शिक्षा नै सशक्तिकरण — “Education is Empowerment.”

History & Present

History

Shree Saraswati Secondary School was established in 2016 B.S. with a mission to provide quality education to the children of Satyawati Rural Municipality. Starting as a primary school, it has grown steadily over the decades, expanding its infrastructure and academic programs to meet the needs of the community. The school has produced countless graduates who now serve in diverse fields across Nepal and abroad.

Present Details

Today, the school serves 245 students from ECD through Grade 12 across two blocks — General Education (ECD to 12, SEE Board) and Technical/Computer Engineering (Grades 9 to 12). Located in Satyawati Rural Municipality–6, Johang, Gulmi, the school is equipped with modern classrooms, a computer lab, and dedicated teaching staff committed to academic excellence and holistic student development.

Our Guiding Principles

Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Gulmi, recognized for academic excellence, strong values, and producing capable, responsible citizens.

Mission

To provide accessible, quality education from early childhood through secondary level, combining general and technical education to prepare students for higher studies and employment.

Values

Discipline, integrity, respect, and a commitment to the holistic development of every student we serve.
