Admissions

How to apply to Shree Saraswati Secondary School

Shree Saraswati Secondary School accepts admissions each academic year across both the General Block (Nursery through Grade 12, SEE Board) and the Technical Block (Computer Engineering, Grade 9-12). Below is an overview of the classes offered and what you'll need to apply.

Classes Offered

Nursery, LKG, UKG, Class 1-10, Class 11 (Science), Class 11 (Management), up to Grade 12.

Documents Required

Student photo, birth certificate, and for higher classes — BLE certificate and previous school transcripts.

Technical Program

Students applying for Computer Engineering (Grade 9-12) apply through the Technical Block at the same campus.

Admission Process

  1. Fill out the online admission form (personal, parent/guardian, address, and academic details).
  2. Upload the required documents — student photo, birth certificate, and BLE certificate/admit card if applicable.
  3. Submit the form. Our office will contact you to confirm your application and next steps.
  4. Visit the campus for document verification and to complete enrollment once your application is confirmed.
